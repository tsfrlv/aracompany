Anne-Birthe Nord

KAS TEVI KUSTINA?

Many things move me some of the time, but two things move me all of the time: the beauty of nature and the stories of people.

I usually have some Coke, yogurt, chocolate, and a banana.

I haven’t yet found a way to dance underwater, on the floor of the sea, but I’d very much like to do so if I can ever find a way!

Flowers always make me smile, especially when they’re from my husband, Patrick

I grew up on a small island off of the west coast of Norway, where the weather is constantly changing and the clouds dance across the sky. We have a saying in Norway: “There’s no such thing as bad weather, just bad clothing.”

Breathe deeply, fill my lungs with the fresh air, and enjoy the beauty of today.
